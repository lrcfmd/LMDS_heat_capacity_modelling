This tool implements a process to calculate the Einstein and Debye contributions to the total heat capacity of a material. Upload a csv file of temperature readings and measured heat capacity, specify the Einstein and Debye temperatures with their pre-factor components to generate tthe resultant contributions across the temperature range. This tool was first reported in The Liverpool Materials Discovery Server: A suite of tools for the collaborative discovery of materials., please consider citing this paper if you use this in your work.
